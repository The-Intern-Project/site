About

Tech internships are kind of a wonderful thing. You get to spend a summer meeting awesome fellow interns, learning about how great companies and early start-ups do their thing, and in general have a great summer.

We've been interns before. We've written about the experience, organized intern events and helped organize intern communities. Internproject.io is an effort to create a single destination for everything required to have a great internship in the Bay Area, curated and maintained to avoid redundancy.

Make sure to sign up for updates to find out when we post more articles as well as hear about events this summer.
