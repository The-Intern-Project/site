The Intern Project


Back for the summer of 2014 to make sure you have an awesome time.


Come meet your fellow interns at the annual Intern Project Summer Kickoff Picnic, the largest intern gathering of the summer! This year, the picnic will be at Yerba Buena Gardens in San Francisco from 2PM - 6PM on Sunday, June 22. Come enjoy great food, fun, games, music, and more! All interns are invited. We're required to have ticketed entry this year, so please RSVP here.

Positive feedback has turned last summer's experiment into a summer mainstay. We'll keep you posted on the dinners and a surprise event planned for the end of the summer. We've also put together a collection of resources and articles below to help you make this summer as great as possible.

The Intern Project kicked off last summer in an effort to connect interns to each other, companies, and the summer's best events. We hosted the first annual picnic, and organized dinners that introduced small groups of interns to influential leaders in tech from around the Bay Area.

Don't miss out on a curated list of the summer's best hackathons, intern meetups, speakers, and startup events!
